## homebrew commands

|commands|explanations|
|:----:|:----:|
|`:T`|Terminal opens below|
|`:VT`|Split opens terminal|
|`:V`|`:vsplit`|
|`:H`|`:resize 0` and move the cursor|
|`:O`|`:resize 15`|
|`:VH`|`:vertical resize 0` and move the cursor|
|`:VO`|`:vertical resize 15`|
|`:B`|binary mode|
|`<C-j>`|bNext|
|`<C-k>`|bnext|

- When you open the terminal you are in insert mode
- Just press \<esc\> in terminal to enter normal mode
- Toggle highlighting by pressing the \<esc\> twice
